The Experience

Flat Camp returns, this time heading to Europe to the countryside right outside Rome, Italy. Unlike a traditional tech conference, this event does not revolve around days jam packed with talks & slides in a big auditorium (not that there’s anything wrong with that). Instead, we’ve taken the “hallway track” and made it the main event. This is the adult version of “summer camp”.

Spend time IRL with the community as we shine a light on our careers, businesses, personal lives, health & wellness, and how the future of Statamic can be shaped to serve us all best.

There will be a casual Q&A session where can dive deep into Statamic's future or past, a show & tell time where you can highlight or feedback on anything you're working on, and spend the rest of our time building relationships, enjoying the grounds of our private Italian villa, and relaxing with a glass of wine.
