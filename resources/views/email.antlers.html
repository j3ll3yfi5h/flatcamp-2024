I've included every single important detail I can think of below, but if you have any questions, just reply to this email and I'll get right back to you. ## The Flat Camp Ethos I hope I've been clear as to what Flat Camp is and what it isn't, but just in case you're jumping in last minute (I know there were a few late sign-ups and ticket swaps), here's a quick rundown. **Flat Camp is a non-formal event.** There's a very loose schedule, no formal classroom style presentations or workshops, and hopefully no pressure on anyone. It's a chance to get away from the grind, enjoy nature and the comradery of other folks in the Statamic community — including the entire dev and support team. Most knowledge transferred in a formal, classroom-style environment only has a 6% retention rate. That feels a bit like a waste of time to us, considering all the other things we could do with the same time. So instead we want to create an environment where you'll remember _96%_ of everything that happened. And we'll do that through conversations, activities, going deeper, getting into practical application in small groups and one-on-ones and simply just _being around each other_, allowing conversations and topics to emerge organically. ## Expectations First off, this event is a brand new thing. While we've put a lot of work into it, we've never run an event like this before so we're all in this together. If you can roll with a few inevitable hiccups and surprises, I'm positive you'll have a great time. I'll be arriving a day early and will do my best to scope everything out and look for any potential issues. If I need to run to the nearest Walmart for something, I'll do it. ## Food If you can eat or drink it, we have it covered.
